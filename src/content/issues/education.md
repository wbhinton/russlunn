Macon County deserves an education system that prepares our children for the challenges of tomorrow. Russ Lunn believes that every student, regardless of their background, should have access to high-quality education. 

Russ will work to:
- Increase teacher pay and retention.
- Modernize school facilities.
- Expand vocational training opportunities.
