Macon County is known for its stunning natural beauty, and it's our responsibility to keep it that way. Russ Lunn understands that sustainable growth is the only way to ensure a prosperous future for our families.

Russ will work to:
- Promote renewable energy initiatives.
- Protect our local water resources.
- Support local farms and sustainable agriculture.
