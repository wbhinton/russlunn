---
title: "Russ Lunn Announces Candidacy for Macon County Commissioner"
publishDate: 2026-03-01
excerpt: "Russ Lunn officially launches his campaign for the Macon County Board of Commissioners, promising a voice for every corner of the county."
---
Russ Lunn announced his candidacy for Macon County Commissioner today, focusing on a platform of education, sustainable growth, and transparency. 

"I am running because I believe in the potential of Macon County," Lunn said to a crowd of supporters. "Working together, we can build a future that is bright for everyone, from the mountains to the valleys."
